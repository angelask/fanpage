A Jagged Edge!

A Jagged Era is the debut album by American R&B Group Jagged Edge released in 1997! Despite a disappointing start,

A Jagged Era peaked at number 104 on the US Billboard 200!

The group is famous for producing slow jams dispite the courtroom drama film for which it was named!

The Rest Of our Lives!

The song the Rest of our lives, a hit from the album, won the Soul Train Music Awards in 2001! It has been a popular song played at weddings across

American Communities all over the world for decades!

One of the more famous weddings that utilized the song was the Strayhorn-Keys Wedding that took place on Valentines day 2019!
